Since the mass shooting at Sandy Hook Elementary School in Newtown, Connecticut, more than 1,700 died in subsequent mass shootings. More than 6,000 suffered injuries in mass shootings.

Since the October 2017 Las Vegas mass shooting, 143 have died in mass shootings and 389+ have been injured, including the 26 killed and 20 injured in the Nov. 5 mass shooting in Texas.
